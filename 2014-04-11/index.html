The goal of entire project was first to understand the structural aspect of the costruction and then produce it as more detailed as possible. First of all I started to decomposing the monument into simple models, developing the main elements; after this I added all the details such as inner walls, glass walls and so on. In the last part of the homework I introduced some urban fittings and some trees.
